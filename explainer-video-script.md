# Explainer Video Script for Compare Agents

## Duration
60-90 seconds

## Goal
A short, friendly explainer that tells users why Compare Agents is useful, what they get from it, and how the service works.

## Script Outline

### 1. Opening (0-15 seconds)
- "Welcome to Compare Agents — the easiest way to find the best local estate agent for your Dublin property."
- "Whether you're selling or letting, our platform helps you compare real agents, fees, and results in one place."

### 2. Why it helps users (15-35 seconds)
- "No more guessing who is best. We bring you up to 4 verified local agents that match your property and goals."
- "See transparent fees, recent performance, and local coverage so you can choose with confidence."

### 3. What users get (35-55 seconds)
- "You get fast matches, clear comparisons, and monthly reporting that helps you stay informed."
- "Our service is free for owners and landlords, and there are no hidden costs."

### 4. How it works (55-80 seconds)
- "Step 1: Share a few quick details about your property and target outcome."
- "Step 2: We match you with top local agents and send their proposals directly to your inbox."
- "Step 3: Compare the proposals, pick the best fit, and work with the agent you trust."

### 5. Closing CTA (80-90 seconds)
- "Get started today and make choosing an agent simple, transparent, and stress-free."
- "Compare Agents — local property agents, better choices, smarter decisions."

## Notes for production
- Keep the tone warm, confident, and straightforward.
- Use on-screen text for the three steps and the main benefits.
- Show the website UI or a mockup of the comparison process if possible.
- End with a clear button or URL prompt: "Start now at Compare Agents."
